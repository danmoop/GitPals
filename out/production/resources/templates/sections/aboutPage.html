What is GitPals?

GitPals helps you to find developers for your project or to take part in someone's project. Cooperative work helps you to dive in to Open-Source projects. You can create portfolio of successful projects by working in a team, what is very appreciated. It's up to you: whether to start your own project and invite people or apply to existing projects. Start taking part in Open-Source development today!
